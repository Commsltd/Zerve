## Sanity Check Validation

âœ… **Healthy Model Indicators:**
- ARR growth 50-150% YoY in early years
- GRR >90%, NRR >100%
- LTV:CAC ratio 3-5x
- CAC payback 6-18 months
- Churn rates declining as tiers mature
- Rule of 40 score >40 by Year 3

âš ï¸ **Warning Signs:**
- ARR growth >200% YoY (unrealistic acquisition)
- GRR <85% (too much churn)
- LTV:CAC <2x (poor unit economics)
- CAC payback >24 months (too expensive to acquire)
- NRR <90% (expansion not covering churn)

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Block shows old data after parameter change  
**Solution:** Re-run the block and all downstream dependencies

**Problem:** ARR looks unrealistic  
**Solution:** Check acquisition volumes and churn rates in `validation.assumptions_check`

**Problem:** Token revenue is zero  
**Solution:** Verify overage rates and PAYG rates are >0 in `inputs.token_economics`

**Problem:** Invoice finance revenue missing  
**Solution:** Check launch timing - institutional starts M18, P2P starts M39

**Problem:** Exports are empty  
**Solution:** Run `calc_engine.output_summary` first, then run export blocks
